 Overview

My research activities mainly focus on automatic indexation and semantic description of audio signals, within the context of artificial intelligence. I also investigate the field of human perception of audiovisual data.

My experience include sound event detection, data visualisation, categorization, as well as automatic analysis of:

  • soundscapes,
  • bioacoustic data,
  • music,
  • gestures,
  • water sounds.

In terms of methods, my current work is primarily sourced from the field of Machine Learning, especially Deep Learning, and from the signal processing domain. In particular, some of my studies focus on low-level descriptors and multi-modal fusion. Some other parts of my work rather rely on experimental psychology.
